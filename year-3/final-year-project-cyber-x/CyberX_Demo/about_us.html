Read About Our Mission. Vision. Plans.

Welcome to CyberX, a British not-for-profit e-learning platform that aims to tackle gender disparity in tech. Our platform is designed to empower girls+ aged 11-17 with free learning tutorials on technology-based subjects, such as coding and OSINT. Our mission is to provide equal opportunities for girls+ to learn and excel in technology, and to bridge the gender gap in the tech industry. At CyberX, we believe that everyone should have access to quality education, regardless of their background or gender. Our platform is designed to be accessible and user-friendly, with a range of tutorials and resources that cater to different learning styles. We are committed to providing a safe and inclusive learning environment, where girls+ can explore their interests and develop their skills in a supportive community. Our team is made up of passionate educators and tech professionals who are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of young girls+. We are constantly updating our platform with new tutorials and resources, and we welcome feedback and suggestions from our users. Join us on our mission to empower the next generation of female tech leaders. Sign up for free today and start learning with CyberX!
